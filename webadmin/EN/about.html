About

This is a server system which enables you to run internet apps such as a blog or federated social network.

Freedombone started in 2013. It was initially based on the Beaglebone Black hardware and inspired by the FreedomBox idea of an inexpensive and simple to manage internet server for the home.

For further information see the website.
